# Cookie Policy

*Last updated: February 2026*

This Cookie Policy explains how The Nordic Deli uses cookies and similar technologies on our website.

---

## What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences and improve your experience.

---

## Cookies We Use

We keep our cookie use minimal. We only use:

### Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function correctly. They cannot be disabled. They include:

- **Cloudflare security cookies** — used by Cloudflare to protect our website from abuse and to verify that you are not a bot (Turnstile challenge).

### Analytics Cookies

We use **Cloudflare Web Analytics** to understand how visitors use our website. This service is privacy-preserving — it does not use cookies to track individuals across sites and does not collect personally identifiable information. No data is shared with advertising networks.

---

## Cookies We Do NOT Use

We do not use:

- Advertising or tracking cookies
- Social media tracking pixels
- Third-party marketing cookies

---

## How to Control Cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

- View what cookies are stored
- Block or allow cookies from specific websites
- Delete all cookies

Blocking essential cookies may affect the functionality of our website.

For more information, visit [allaboutcookies.org](https://www.allaboutcookies.org).
